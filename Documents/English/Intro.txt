It is the year 2107 AD, and mankind has reached out to the stars.

After decades of exploration, discovering nothing but dead rock,
our quest for a second Earth has finally come to an end.

A terrestrial planet in the Nashira system has become the symbol
for humanity's hopes and dreams of a new paradise.

The most powerful factions, distinct in their ideologies and beliefs,
have sent expeditionary forces to stake their claim to this new world.
